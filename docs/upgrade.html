Common Questions

How does billing work?

After you submit your request, the LifeVine team will reach out personally to set up your account and handle billing directly. We use Stripe for secure payment processing. Your card information is never shared through the app.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Email hello@lifevine.app anytime and we'll take care of it.

What happens to my profile if I downgrade?

Your profile and all content stays. You'll return to the Free plan limits — extra events and opportunities beyond the free tier will be archived (not deleted) and reactivated if you upgrade again.

Is the free plan really free forever?

Yes. The Free tier has no expiration and no credit card required. It exists because we believe every organization that wants to serve their community deserves a presence on LifeVine.

I'm not a contributor yet. How do I get listed?

Open the LifeVine app, go to Profile, and tap "Become a Contributor" to submit your organization for review. Approval typically takes 1–2 business days.
